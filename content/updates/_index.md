## 2024

- **August 2024** — Presented **[SIBling](https://naomibaes.github.io/publication/journal-article_2024_acl_sibling/)** at **ACL 2024** in Bangkok, Thailand.
- **June 2024** — Co-authored a public-facing [article](https://theconversation.com/mental-illness-psychiatric-disorder-or-psychological-problem-what-should-we-call-mental-distress-226748) with Professor Nick Haslam in *The Conversation* on what we should call mental distress — and why the words we use matter. Companion to our [*British Journal of Social Psychology* paper](https://bpspsychub.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/abs/10.1111/bjso.12713) using folk theories to uncover shared conceptions, with Dr Melissa Wheeler, Associate Professor Samuel Wilson, and Dr Vlad Demsar.
